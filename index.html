About Me

Allow me to introduce myself. My name is Scott Sutton, and I began my foray into web development, converting procedure manuals into HTML Help format. When the authors of these manuals tested the new product and experienced its interactive nature, they requested I also construct websites for them to post their documentation. Over time as my skill level progressed, I incorporated web forms and web applications into the range of services I could deliver.

A few years back, I took a break from coding to investigate other interests. I returned to school and earned an accounting certificate, graduating with honors. After completing an internship alongside a local bookkeeper, I realized just how much I missed programming and the creative outlet it provides. As a result, I adjusted my career ambitions to facilitate reentry into the technical landscape.

I immediately started taking online courses, immersing myself in several popular front‑ and back‑end languages. Then, I applied the knowledge acquired to a portfolio of personal projects. Later, I connected with local business owners to address some of their needs through custom coding solutions.
